## Weighting method (v2 — confidence overlay)
1. Anchor each weight to composite/conviction (v1).
2. **Overlay: scale by Senior-Analyst confidence — High ×1.0, Med ×0.85, Low ×0.6.**
3. Renormalize equities to ~90%; re-apply guardrails: **max single name 7.5% · max theme 35% · max correlated cluster 15%**.
4. Residual that can't be deployed without breaching a cluster cap flows to **cash**.

**Why cash rose to ~12%:** the overlay concentrates into High-confidence names, but the water trio (XYL/ECL/AWK) and 2 of 3 EM-fintech names (NU/RELY) are all High-confidence — so the overlay pushed those clusters back to the 15% cap. Capping them (rather than over-concentrating) leaves the surplus in cash. This is the intended, honest behavior: *conviction can't be expressed past the diversification limits, so it sits in cash.*
